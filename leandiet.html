Lean Diet

Diet Plan for Bodybuilding (Ages 18-50): Caloric Intake: Adjust caloric intake based on individual goals, activity level, and metabolism. Macronutrient Distribution: Follow a balanced macronutrient distribution to support muscle growth and overall health. Carbohydrates: 45-65% of total calories Protein: 10-35% of total calories Fat: 20-35% of total calories Sample Meal Plan: Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole grain toast Greek yogurt with berries Mid-Morning Snack: Protein shake with almond milk and banana Handful of almonds Lunch: Grilled chicken breast or tofu with quinoa and mixed vegetables Side salad with olive oil and balsamic vinegar dressing Afternoon Snack: Cottage cheese with sliced peaches Carrot sticks with hummus Pre-Workout Meal: Brown rice with grilled salmon or tempeh Steamed broccoli or asparagus Post-Workout Meal: Protein smoothie with whey protein powder, spinach, and pineapple Whole grain crackers with tuna or chicken salad Dinner: Lean beef or lentil chili with whole grain bread Mixed green salad with avocado slices Evening Snack: Greek yogurt with sliced strawberries Peanut butter on rice cakes
