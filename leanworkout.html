Lean Workout

Day 1: Chest and Triceps Exercise 1: Bench Press Sets: 4 Reps: 8-10 Exercise 2: Incline Dumbbell Press Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 3: Chest Flyes Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 4: Tricep Dips Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 5: Tricep Pushdowns Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Day 2: Back and Biceps Exercise 1: Deadlifts Sets: 4 Reps: 6-8 Exercise 2: Pull-Ups Sets: 3 Reps: 8-10 Exercise 3: Barbell Rows Sets: 3 Reps: 8-10 Exercise 4: Dumbbell Curls Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 5: Hammer Curls Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery Rest day or engage in light cardiovascular activity such as walking, cycling, or yoga for recovery. Day 4: Legs Exercise 1: Squats Sets: 4 Reps: 8-10 Exercise 2: Romanian Deadlifts Sets: 3 Reps: 8-10 Exercise 3: Leg Press Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 4: Lunges Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 5: Calf Raises Sets: 4 Reps: 12-15 Day 5: Shoulders and Abs Exercise 1: Shoulder Press Sets: 4 Reps: 8-10 Exercise 2: Lateral Raises Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 3: Front Raises Sets: 3 Reps: 10-12 Exercise 4: Planks Sets: 3 Duration: 30-60 seconds Exercise 5: Russian Twists Sets: 3 Reps: 12-15 (each side) Day 6: Rest or Active Recovery Rest day or engage in light cardiovascular activity and stretching for recovery. Day 7: Cardio and Core Cardiovascular Exercise: Duration: 30-60 minutes Choose activities such as running, cycling, swimming, or HIIT workouts. Core Exercises: Perform a circuit of core exercises including crunches, bicycle crunches, leg raises, and mountain climbers. Sets: 3 Reps: 12-15 (each exercise) Notes: Warm up before each workout with 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretches. Cool down after each workout with 5-10 minutes of static stretches targeting the muscles worked. Progressively increase the weight or intensity of exercises as strength and fitness improve. Ensure proper form and technique for each exercise to prevent injuries.
